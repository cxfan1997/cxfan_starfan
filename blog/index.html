Personal Writing Tips

Posted on 2021-09-12 Edited on 2021-09-20 In Miscellaneous

From solar farming project SW part - ERL

  • Do not trust reference management software! Check the references one by one!
  • Be more specific! Be more specific! Be more specific! Describe the figures first, element by element, but not irrevalant features. Then derive the conclusion.

From solar farming project LW part - 2nd version draft

  • Do not sound too assertive. Avoid using "challenge". This study has been challenged by... => This study is not consistent with...
  • Avoid using "In sth, ..." or "As for sth, ...". Try using sth as the subject of the sentence. As for the solar farm, it has a lower surface reflectance => The solar farm has a lower surface reflectance.
  • When comparing the same property of different objects, remember to compare "apple to apple". Do not omit the property of the latter object. The temperature of the solar panel is higher than the ground => The temperature of the solar panel is higher than that of the ground.
  • Directly write the name of the object rather than the category name. It used a novel algorithm, namely Temperature Emissivity Separation (TES) algorithm, to... => It used Temperature Emissivity Separation (TES) algorithm to...
  • Check the digits of the floats! Check the digits of the floats! Check the digits of the floats! Use the uncertainty to get the sense of how many digits you need!
