Fall 2020 Week 17 Progress - December 20th

Posted on 2020-12-20 Edited on 2020-12-19 In Weekly Progress

Plan for the Next Few Weeks

  • A Devil schedule will be implemented strictly.
  • Failure to comply with the regulations will lead to severe penalty.

Weekly Notes

The Fall 2020 term has passed. Most efforts have been poured into the courses. However, the research is completely dismissed, which resulted in a severe impact on the satisfaction of the research progress. Instead of attributing to the lack of well-designed plan to carry out the research, the situation is indeed due to the lazy, sloppy, and careless attitude. Therefore, several actions will be taken in the next few weeks to serve as both a penalization and a warning.

Devil Schedule

The daily schedule will be adjusted. The key points will be summarized below.

  • Starting Dec 20, the regular daily schedule will be strictly enforced, as shown in the Monthly Plan of December. The progress will be reflected in the daily habit tracker. Any incompliance with the original plan will lead to all points taken off, whatever the reason.
  • The wake-up time will be adjusted 30 minutes ahead of schedule. That is, the morning alarm rings off at 6 AM every day.
  • The English TED talk watching session will be placed between 6 AM to 6:30 AM instead of in the evening.
  • The breakfast should be finished as soon as possible. It is not allowed to use mobile phone except for the family video chat. Cleaning should be done before 7:55 AM.
  • The morning reading session will be cancelled. All morning sessions will be used for main tasks.
  • The lunch should be finished as soon as possible. It is not allowed to use mobile phone at any circumstance. Cleaning should be done before 12:50 PM.
  • Due to the cold weather, the daily exercise session will be replaced by a evening walk in Northwood for about 15 minutes.
  • The keyboard piano session will be shortened to 30 minutes a day.
  • The bedtime will be delayed until 11 PM.

  • That certain screenshot will be used as the wallpaper on mobile phone and PCs.
  • That certain screenshot will be printed out and pasted around the house.
