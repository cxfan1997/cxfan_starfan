Satellite Observation of Aerosol-Precipitation Relationship
The adjustment of cloud amount to aerosol effects occurs to a large extent in response to the aerosol effect on precipitation. This is studied here for marine boundary layer water clouds by analyzing the dependence of rain intensity, as measured by the radar onboard the Global Precipitation Measurement (GPM), on cloud properties as measured by MODIS.
